# New Hire Onboarding

Welcome to Northwind Co. Your first two weeks are structured to get you set up with accounts,
equipment, and an initial team context.

## Week 1

- **Day 1:** HR orientation (9:00–11:00). Laptop pickup and account setup. Meet your manager for a
  1:1 at 1:00 PM.
- **Day 2:** Security training (self-paced, ~3 hours) and benefits enrollment deadline.
- **Day 3–5:** Team introductions, codebase walkthrough with your onboarding buddy, shadow a
  standup.

## Week 2

- Complete at least one small starter task reviewed by your onboarding buddy.
- Attend the company-wide New Hire Welcome session (Thursdays, 10:00 AM).
- Schedule coffee chats with three cross-functional partners recommended by your manager.

## 30/60/90 Day Goals

Your manager will provide a written 30/60/90 day plan by the end of week 2. This plan should
include at least one measurable goal per month and a documented review point with HR at 90 days.

## Questions

For HR questions, contact `people@northwind.example`. For technical setup, file a ticket with IT
through the internal portal. Your onboarding buddy is the first stop for any team-specific question.
