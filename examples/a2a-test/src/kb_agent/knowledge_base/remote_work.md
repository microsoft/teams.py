# Remote Work Guidelines

Northwind Co. operates a hybrid work model. Employees are expected to be in the office at least
two days per week, typically Tuesday and Thursday, unless granted a fully-remote exception.

## Home Office Stipend

New employees receive a one-time $1,500 home office stipend to be used within the first 90 days of
employment. The stipend may be used for desks, chairs, monitors, keyboards, mice, and noise-canceling
headphones. Stipend purchases are tracked through the Finance portal.

## Equipment

All employees are issued a company laptop. Monitors, keyboards, and webcams for home use are
considered part of the home office stipend and are not separately provisioned. In-office
workstations are equipped by IT.

## Internet and Utilities

Internet and utility costs incurred at home are not reimbursed under standard employment. Employees
on a fully-remote arrangement receive a $60/month connectivity allowance, reported as taxable
income.

## Time Zones

All employees are expected to overlap at least four hours per day with the Seattle headquarters
time zone (Pacific Time), regardless of remote status.
