# PTO Policy

Northwind Co. employees accrue paid time off (PTO) at a rate of 1.5 days per month for the first
three years of employment, and 2 days per month thereafter. PTO can be used for vacation, personal
days, or illness.

## Requesting PTO

Submit PTO requests through the HR portal at least two weeks in advance for vacations longer than
three days. Sick leave does not require advance notice but must be reported to your manager before
the start of your workday.

## Carryover

Up to 10 unused PTO days may be carried over into the next calendar year. Days beyond 10 are
forfeited on January 1. There is no option to cash out unused PTO except upon separation from the
company.

## Holidays

Company holidays are in addition to PTO and include New Year's Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day,
Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, the day after Thanksgiving, and December 24-26.
