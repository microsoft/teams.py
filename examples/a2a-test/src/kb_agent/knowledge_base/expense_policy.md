# Expense Policy

Northwind Co. reimburses reasonable business expenses incurred in the course of employment. All
expense reports must be submitted through the Finance portal within 30 days of the expense date.

## Meals and Travel

- **Meals during travel:** up to $75/day in the US, $100/day internationally. Itemized receipts
  required for any single meal over $25.
- **Lodging:** book through the approved corporate travel tool. Hotel upgrades are not reimbursed.
- **Flights:** economy class for all flights under 6 hours; premium economy permitted for flights
  over 6 hours with manager approval.
- **Ground transportation:** rideshare (Uber/Lyft) or taxis are reimbursed; rental cars require
  pre-approval.

## Client Entertainment

Client meals and entertainment up to $200/person require no pre-approval. Above that threshold,
obtain written approval from your director before the event.

## Non-reimbursable

Alcohol outside of client entertainment, personal entertainment (movies, spa services), traffic
fines, and upgrades to first/business class are not reimbursable.
