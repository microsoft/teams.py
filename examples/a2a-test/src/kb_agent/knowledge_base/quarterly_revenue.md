# Quarterly Revenue

Northwind Co. publishes quarterly revenue totals by region. Figures are in USD thousands.

## FY2024 by Region

| Quarter | North | South | East | West |
|---------|-------|-------|------|------|
| Q1 | 1240 | 890 | 1510 | 720 |
| Q2 | 1380 | 950 | 1680 | 780 |
| Q3 | 1425 | 1020 | 1720 | 810 |
| Q4 | 1590 | 1105 | 1840 | 900 |

## Notes

- East region has been the top revenue producer for six consecutive quarters.
- West region growth accelerated in H2 2024 after the Seattle team restructure.
- All FY2024 figures are unaudited through Q3; Q4 is preliminary.
